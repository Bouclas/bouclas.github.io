About me

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Crete, in the Department of Computer Science. In 2014, I earned the Diploma of Engineering at the School of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Technical University of Crete, Greece. I completed my undergraduate thesis under the supervision of Professor M. Zervakis who also supervised my Master of Science degree at the same department, which I received at 2016. Currently, I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science, University of Crete, under the supervision of Professor Antonis Argyros. I am also working as Graduate Student Research Assistant, ICS Forth, with the Computational Vision and Robotics Laboratory (CVRL).
